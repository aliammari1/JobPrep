# University Career Center Case Study

## 🎓 Tech State University Career Center Transformation

### Institution Profile
- **Institution**: Tech State University (TSU)
- **Location**: Austin, Texas
- **Students**: 12,000 total, 2,400 Computer Science & Engineering
- **Career Center Staff**: 6 counselors
- **Previous Placement Rate**: 68% within 6 months
- **Challenge**: Scale career services to meet growing student demand

## 📊 Initial Situation (Fall 2024)

### Challenges Faced

1. **Limited Resources**
   - 6 counselors for 2,400 CS students
   - Average 1:400 counselor-to-student ratio
   - Only 30-minute slots available per student
   - 3-week waiting list for appointments

2. **Inconsistent Preparation**
   - Students received varying quality advice
   - No standardized interview practice
   - Limited technical interview guidance
   - No objective skill assessment

3. **Low FAANG Placement**
   - Only 8% of students received FAANG offers
   - Students lacked confidence for top-tier companies
   - Limited exposure to system design
   - Weak behavioral interview skills

4. **CV Quality Issues**
   - Generic resumes not optimized for ATS
   - Missing quantifiable achievements
   - Poor formatting
   - Not tailored to specific companies

5. **Tracking & Analytics**
   - No way to measure student progress
   - Difficult to identify students needing help
   - Limited data for program improvement
   - Manual tracking in spreadsheets

### Baseline Metrics (Fall 2024)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| 6-month Placement Rate | 68% |
| FAANG Offers | 8% |
| Average Starting Salary | $78,000 |
| Students Using Career Center | 42% |
| Student Satisfaction | 6.8/10 |
| Interview Success Rate | 31% |
| Time to First Offer | 4.2 months |

## 🚀 Implementation of JobPrep AI (Spring 2025)

### Phase 1: Pilot Program (January - March 2025)

#### Setup
- **Cohort**: 100 senior CS students
- **License**: Enterprise plan ($5/student/semester)
- **Integration**: Required for capstone course
- **Support**: 1 career counselor as JobPrep champion

#### Requirements
- Minimum 10 mock interviews per semester
- 20 code challenges completed
- CV created and reviewed
- Analytics reviewed with counselor

#### Training
- 2-hour workshop for students
- 1-day training for counselors
- Weekly office hours
- Slack channel for support

### Phase 2: Full Rollout (Fall 2025)

#### Expansion
- **Students**: All 2,400 CS/Engineering students
- **Integration Points**:
  - Required in 300-level courses
  - Integrated into career prep seminar
  - Available for underclassmen (optional)
  - Alumni access (graduated within 1 year)

#### Resource Allocation
- **Budget**: $12,000/semester ($5 per student)
- **Staff**: 1 dedicated JobPrep coordinator
- **Counselors**: All 6 trained on platform
- **Student Ambassadors**: 10 power users

## 📈 Implementation Strategy

### Curriculum Integration

**CS 301: Professional Development (Required)**
- Week 1-2: JobPrep onboarding, CV building
- Week 3-4: Behavioral interview practice (5 sessions)
- Week 5-8: Technical interviews (10 sessions)
- Week 9-10: System design for seniors
- Week 11-12: Real interview simulations
- Week 13-14: Reflection and analytics review

**CS 401: Senior Capstone**
- Ongoing interview practice
- Weekly code challenges
- Job application tracking
- Peer interview practice using JobPrep

**Career Prep Workshop Series**
- Monthly workshops using JobPrep data
- Best practices from high performers
- Company-specific preparation sessions
- Alumni panel discussions

### Counselor Workflow Enhancement

## 💡 Key Success Factors

### What Worked

1. **Mandatory Integration**
   - Required usage drove adoption
   - Students couldn't "forget" to prepare
   - Built practice habits early

2. **Counselor Buy-In**
   - Early training and support
   - Champions helped evangelize
   - Data proved value quickly

3. **Data-Driven Approach**
   - Analytics informed counselor sessions
   - Identified at-risk students early
   - Measured progress objectively

4. **Peer Learning**
   - Student ambassadors
   - Success stories shared
   - Friendly competition

5. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Monthly feedback sessions
   - Iterative curriculum updates
   - Responsive to student needs

### Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge 1: Initial Resistance**
- Some students thought it was "extra work"
- **Solution**: Showed data on improved outcomes, made it required, celebrated early wins

**Challenge 2: Technical Issues**
- Some students had connectivity problems
- **Solution**: Campus computer labs with guaranteed bandwidth, offline practice mode

**Challenge 3: Equity Concerns**
- Not all students had same tech access
- **Solution**: Loaner laptops, lab hours, mobile app

**Challenge 4: Over-Reliance**
- Some students only used JobPrep, ignored counselors
- **Solution**: Required counselor check-ins, emphasized complementary nature

## 🎯 Best Practices Developed

### For Students

**Semester Plan Template**:
```
Freshman/Sophomore:
- 5 behavioral interviews per semester
- 10 code challenges per week
- Basic CV creation
- Attend career fairs

Junior:
- 10 interviews per semester
- 15 code challenges per week
- Technical interview focus
- 1 internship secured

Senior (Fall):
- 15+ interviews
- System design practice
- Company research
- Active applications

Senior (Spring):
- Interview sprint
- Negotiation practice
- Decision framework
- Offer evaluation
```

### For Counselors

**Effective Student Meeting Agenda** (30 min):
1. Review analytics dashboard (5 min)
2. Discuss specific improvement areas (10 min)
3. Goal setting for next 2 weeks (5 min)
4. Career strategy discussion (10 min)

**Red Flag Indicators**:
- <3 practice interviews by mid-semester
- Low confidence scores
- Declining engagement
- No applications started (seniors)
